LifeLink

Emergency Blood Supply Network Connecting Donors, Hospitals, and Patients in Real-Time

Our Mission

LifeLink is a revolutionary platform designed to save lives by addressing the critical challenges of blood donation and emergency blood supply. Every second counts, and LifeLink ensures that compatible blood is located and delivered swiftly.

Our goal is to reduce the effort and time required to locate compatible blood so that no patient suffers due to unavailability.

Key Features

Real-Time Inventory

Monitor blood availability across multiple blood banks instantly.

Emergency Requests

Send instant blood requests with location-based matching to nearby donors.

Donor Management

Maintain up-to-date donor health records and availability status.

Automated Notifications

Notify compatible donors via SMS and email during emergencies.

Blood Drive Scheduling

Plan and manage community blood donation events efficiently.

Analytics Dashboard

Gain insights into donation trends and identify shortages.

Who We Serve

Hospitals & Clinics: Quick access to compatible blood supplies.

Blood Banks: Efficient inventory and donor management.

Registered Donors: Save lives in emergencies.

NGOs & Organizations: Organize impactful blood donation events.
